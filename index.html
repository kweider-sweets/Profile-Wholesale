A Legacy of Damascene Luxury

Since 1969, Kweider has been the custodian of authentic Damascene artisanship. From the heart of Syria to London, we have spent decades perfecting the art of fine Arabic sweets, blending time-honored traditions with uncompromising premium quality.

We are delighted to present this exclusive portfolio, thoughtfully curated to showcase our signature collections and luxurious packaging. This document is designed to give you a glimpse into our world of elegance and exceptional taste, setting the stage for what we can achieve together.
